Harvest Geek Base Station V1.2
Engineer: Rowan Gifford
Contact: rowan.gifford@gmail.com
Date: 26th May 2015

2 layer PTH 1.6mm FR4 1 oz Cu I.S.

PCB size: 101mm x 57.72mm

Files:

BASESTATION_BOARD_OUTLINE.gbr - PCB outline, standard 2mm router, no panalisation
BASESTATION_BOTTOM_MASK.gbs - Bottom layer solder mask
BASESTATION_BOTTOM_COPPER.gbl - Bottom copper layer
BASESTATION_TOP_MASK.gts - Top layer solder mask
BASESTATION_TOP_COPPER.gtl - Top copper layer
BASESTATION_TOP_PASTE.gtp - Top solde paste layer (no paste on bottom layer)
BASESTATION_TOP_SILK.gto - Top layer silkscreen (no silkscreen on bottom layer)
BASESTATION_DRILL.drl - Drill file, all plated through hole.
BASESTATION_TOP_ASSY.pdf - Top assembly file, shows component placement and references for population
BASESTATION_BOTTOM_ASSY.pdf - Bottom assembly file, shows component placement and references for population. Shown FLIPPED (i.e. bottom side facing)
BASESTATION_TOP_POS.pos - Top layer pick and place position file. Origin 0,0 is located at fiducial FID1. Zero orientation follows IPC-7351C Level A (Pin 1 in Upper left). No SMD on bottom layer.
